📖 How to Play

Goal: Be the first to play all your cards.

Matching: Match the top card by color or number.

Special Cards:

  • Skip ⊘ — Next player misses a turn
  • Reverse ⇄ — Reverses turn order
  • +2 — Next player draws 2 cards
  • Wild W — Change the color
  • +4 — Change color + next draws 4

UNO: Press the UNO button when you have 1 card left!

Scoring: First to reach the target score wins the match.
