ℹ️ How to Play

Goal: Be first to play all your cards.

Match: Play a card matching the color or number of the top card.

Special Cards:

• Skip (⊘) — Next player misses a turn

• Reverse (⇄) — Changes play direction

• +2 — Next player draws 2 cards

• Wild (★) — Choose any color

• +4 — Choose color + next player draws 4

UNO: Press the UNO button when you have 1 card left!

Scoring: Winner gets points from opponents' hands. First to 500 wins!
